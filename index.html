What's project-euler ?

Project Euler (named after Leonhard Euler) is a website dedicated to a series of computational problems intended to be solved with computer programs. The project attracts adults and students interested in mathematics and computer programming.

Participants can track their progress through achievement levels based on the number of problems solved. A new level is reached for every 25 problems solved.


什么是“欧拉计划” ？

欧拉计划(Project Euler) 是一个解题网站，站内提供了一系列数学题供用户解答，解题的用户主要是对数学和计算机编程感兴趣的成年人及学生。目前该站包含了四百多道不同难度的数学题，每一题都可以通过计算机程序在1分钟内求出结果。
